The Golden Age of Video Games

The history of video games started as early as the 1950’s But did not become popular until the 70s and 80s, when arcade games were first introduced.

The golden age of arcade video games was from 1978 to 1982.

This was the beginning of the popularity of arcades and video game pop culture. Since the 1980’s it has become a popular entertainment outlet and received a pop culture following that remained vast and ever growing.
